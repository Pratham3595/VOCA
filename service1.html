Voice Navigation

Voca offers a powerful voice navigation feature that allows users to control and explore the website using simple voice commands. This functionality is especially helpful for users with physical disabilities, enabling them to navigate without the need for a mouse or keyboard.

You can say commands like “scroll down” or “scroll up” to move through the page, creating a hands-free, accessible experience. This voice interaction makes digital browsing more intuitive, inclusive, and seamless for all users.
