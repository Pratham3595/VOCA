Voice Form Filling

Voca provides a smart voice form filling feature that allows users to complete online forms simply by speaking. Instead of typing each response manually, users can dictate their answers, and the system accurately converts speech into text, automatically placing it into the appropriate fields.

This feature is especially beneficial for users with physical impairments or those looking for a faster, hands-free input method. It enhances accessibility, saves time, and ensures a smoother, more inclusive interaction with digital forms.
